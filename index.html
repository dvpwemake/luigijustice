Justice for Luigi Mangione


Why We Fundraise:

Whether you believe Luigi Mangione is guilty or not, or his actions were justified or not, justice must be served. It's not just about one case; it's about ensuring justice for everyone. When justice fails, it seeks other ways to manifest.

Fair Trial for Luigi:

No matter one's view on his actions, Luigi Mangione deserves a trial that upholds the principles of justice and fairness. Your support ensures that he receives a legal process free from bias, setting a standard for equitable treatment under the law.

Justice for All:

This case transcends the individual; it's about reinforcing the justice system for everyone. Your donation helps advocate for a legal system where justice is impartial, ensuring that every person, regardless of status, has their day in court.

Equalizing Power Malpractice:

Beyond the courtroom, there's a broader fight for equality against both government overreach and manipulative corporate practices. Your contribution supports initiatives that challenge these abuses, promoting accountability and reform to protect the public interest.

How can I support Justice Course

Contributing to Luigi Justice fund, raising awareness on social media, and advocating for systemic changes in healthcare and corporate accountability.

1. Financial contribution

Support Luigi Justice by donating to 501c(3) charity fund aimed at ensuring he and people like him receives a fair trial, with contributions currently being coordinated through AppChurch Global Foundation.

2. Spread words, raise awareness

Use social media platforms like X, Instagram, or TikTok to share information, posts, and hashtags like #LuigiJuestice to increase visibility. Engage with content or create your own to discuss the broader implications of the case on social justice and healthcare reform.

3. Educate and discuss

Talk About the Case: Educate others about the complexities of the case, focusing on issues like healthcare ethics, corporate accountability, and the right to a fair trial. Discussions can be held in community forums, educational settings, or through content creation like blogs or videos.

4. Support broader causes

Advocate for Healthcare Reform: Use this case as a catalyst to advocate for broader healthcare reform. Support organizations like LuigiJustice that fight for healthcare rights, transparency, and accountability in the insurance and other sectors.
